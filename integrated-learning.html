Integrated Learning & Projects

This section highlights how I have applied theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios through research, internships, and interdisciplinary projects.

Moog Inc. ‚Äì Design, Analysis & Power Flow

While interning at Moog Military Aircraft, I worked on modules used in Black Hawk and Sea Hawk helicopters. It was a proud moment to contribute engineering to platforms of this scale and national importance. Tasks are non-discloseable but I can show you epic pictures of the choppers.

I got a good mix of designing power modules, testing hardware for remote motor control units, statistical analysis, and troubleshooting real problems with aircraft electronics.

Fisher Price: Debug Tool

As part of the Thomas the Tank Engine team, I developed a scanning debug tool for the embedded systems inside the toys. I made this tool to simplify data collection and help QA technicians isolate problems in returned toys or toys in-store.

Thomas the Tank: Firmware Analysis

My main project at fisher price was working with the thomas team to create a product reading tool. It was an a fun experience to work on a toy that I spent my childhood playing with

Tapecon ‚Äì Flexible Hybrid Electronics

I joined Tapecon during their push to become a leader in FHE (Flexible Hybrid Electronics). I designed circuits, tested conductive inks, and helped onboard new manufacturing equipment including a large-scale CNC laser system. Weekly technical presentations kept my designs aligned with product goals.

Circuit Prototyping

I prototyped dozens of analog and digital circuits for Tapecon, including logic gates, oscillators, and sensor interfaces. My skills in breadboarding, simulation, and CAD proved crucial for rapid iteration. I also worked on printing these designs on flexible substrates.

Home Server Project

I designed and built a custom Network Attached Storage system using an IBM System x3650 M3 server. This project challenged my understanding of hardware interfaces, RAID configurations, and Linux-based file system management. Once finished it will become an essential part of my home lab.

"Garden of Amnesia" Auto Watering System (with Sarah Hennessy)

I collaborated with Sarah Hennessy. on a fully automated watering project using sensors and pumps. The system dynamically adjusts based on moisture readings. We prototyped with Arduino and integrated circuit relays.
